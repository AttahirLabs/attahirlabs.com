Source Discipline

No installability theater

The page now separates live conversion paths from internal pipeline status, so visitors are not asked to trust claims the public product surface cannot support yet.

How we describe products

Public claims are tied to current app status: TariffShield and StockClearance are presented as installable, free tools are presented as usable, and the remaining apps are described as pipeline products with pending App Store materials.

We also avoid blanket compliance promises on the homepage. Privacy and legal details stay in the policy and app-specific flows where they can be kept accurate.
