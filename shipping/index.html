How the retail shipping estimate works

This calculator uses clear constants for route zones, dimensional weight, retail base rates, per-pound pricing, delivery-speed multipliers, order-value surcharges, and a small handling buffer. It is meant for planning and benchmarking before you quote a shopper or compare fulfillment options.

What to enter

  • Origin and destination: country plus postal or ZIP code for the shipping lane.
  • Weight and dimensions: carriers often charge the greater of actual and dimensional weight.
  • Order value: higher-value parcels may need more careful handling or included coverage.
  • Speed preference: compare lower-cost, balanced, and urgent retail benchmarks.
