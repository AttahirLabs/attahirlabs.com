Data boundaries

Built for product-level inventory decisions.

StockClearance uses merchant product, inventory, discount, and order-line signals only to support inventory analytics and clearance workflows.

When order data is available

The app can use order dates, line items, product IDs, quantities, and line-item revenue to improve last-sale timing, units sold, velocity, seasonal history, and clearance-action results.

When order data is not available

The app falls back to inventory age and stock-level signals, and it tells the merchant when sales velocity is unavailable instead of hiding that limitation.

What it does not do

StockClearance does not need customer names, emails, addresses, payment details, or customer profiles for its inventory analytics.

FAQ

Common merchant questions.

Is this a purchasing or ERP system?

No. StockClearance is the focused clearance layer for Shopify merchants who want a cleaner dead-stock and markdown workflow without replacing their whole operations stack.

Can it create discounts?

Yes. StockClearance can apply flash-sale price markdowns and create Shopify automatic discounts for selected bundle suggestions.

Can it guarantee recovered revenue?

No. The app helps merchants act earlier and track results, but it does not promise that a weak SKU will sell.
