What this import duty calculator helps you estimate

This free calculator is for one question: what will this shipment really cost once duty is added? It estimates tariff rate, duty amount, and total landed cost based on origin country, destination market, product value, shipping, and insurance.

Your total landed cost is the number that matters for pricing and margin. That's product cost + shipping + insurance + import duties, plus any other fees not already baked into the quote.

Quick answer

If you're comparing suppliers or deciding whether a product still works after tariffs, start with the calculator, then sanity-check the result against your broker or freight forwarder if the shipment is large or classification-sensitive.

What is CIF Value?

CIF stands for Cost, Insurance, and Freight. Most countries calculate import duty on the CIF value — not just the product cost. That means your shipping and insurance costs increase the duty you pay. Our calculator accounts for this automatically.
